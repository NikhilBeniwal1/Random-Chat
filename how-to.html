Account Deletion Process

  1. Step 1: Go to Settings

    Navigate to the settings section of your account.

  2. Step 2: Click on Log Out

    Click the "Log Out" button to log out of your account.

  3. Step 3: Log In Again

    Log in to your account again with your credentials.

  4. Step 4: Go to Settings Again

    Once logged in, navigate back to the settings section.

  5. Step 5: Click on Delete Account Permanently

    Click the "Delete Account Permanently" option to delete your account.
