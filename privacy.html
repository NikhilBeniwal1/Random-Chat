Last Updated: 30/09/2024

We may use the information we collect to:

We do not sell or rent your Personal Information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your Personal Information at any time. To do so, please contact us at privacy@randomchatapp.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the App will be considered as acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@randomchatapp.com.
